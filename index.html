LuckyWheel Pro — Free Spin Wheel, QR Prize Code & Lucky Box

LuckyWheel Pro is a free, browser-based event tool with three powerful features: Spin Wheel to pick a random winner from up to 1,000 participants, QR Prize Code for hidden prize reveals via smartphone scan, and Lucky Box for opening surprise gift boxes at your event. Built by Sutahhar Jegatheesan · msdevbuild.com.

Perfect for corporate events, schools, colleges, family functions, weddings, conferences and festival celebrations in Malaysia, Singapore, India, Indonesia, and worldwide. Featuring 14 festival themes: Diwali, Chinese New Year, Hari Raya Aidilfitri, Deepavali, Christmas, Holi, Birthday, Halloween, New Year, Malaysia Merdeka, School/Academic and more. All data saved in your browser — no account, no server, completely free.
